Instructions for Photo Booth:

- Click "Take Photos" button and wait for camera to load.
- Take 4 photos (if you take more than 4, the last 4 will be used).
- Close the camera app ("X" in top right corner).
- Click "View Photostrip" to view or "Print Photostrip" to print your photos.
    - If you clicked "View", close the preview before printing.
- Please do not close the Photo Booth app window.
